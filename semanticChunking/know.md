semantic chunking is type of chunking where we devide text based on the semantic meaning of sentences 


like we do embedding of all the sentences of the text and find similarities then group sentences whose similarities are
higher then the threshold and make chunk of that.


in ai-meet-ass project i first do splitting based on the sections and then done the semantic chunking (this gives better result then direct semantic chunking)

used advanced-chunker library
link: https://github.com/rango-ramesh/advanced-chunker
